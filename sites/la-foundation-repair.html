Warning Signs

These symptoms mean your foundation needs immediate attention

↕️

Cracks in Walls

Diagonal cracks from door corners and stair-step cracks in brick are classic foundation settlement indicators.

🚪

Sticking Doors

Doors and windows that stick, won't close, or have gaps at the top indicate the home is shifting.

📐

Sloping Floors

Noticeable slope or bounce in your floors signals that the slab or supports beneath are settling unevenly.

💧

Water in Crawl Space

Standing water or moisture under your home accelerates wood rot and undermines the foundation.
