Privacy Policy

Last updated: 17 July 2026

This policy is a plain-language starting point that describes how the website currently works. Please have it reviewed by qualified legal counsel and confirm it matches your actual data practices before relying on it.

Who we are

Secret Systems LLC ("Secret Systems," "we," "us") operates the website at secretsystems.io. You can reach us at info@secretsystems.io or (337) 258-8818.

Information we collect

We collect information you choose to give us through our contact form, including your name, business name, phone number, email address, current website, the service you're interested in, an optional revenue range, and the details you type into the message field. If you email or call us directly, we receive whatever you send.

When website analytics are enabled, we may also collect standard, non-identifying usage information (such as pages visited and general device/browser type) to understand how the site is used.

How we use it

  • To respond to your inquiry and provide a quote or consultation.
  • To communicate with you about services you've asked about.
  • To understand and improve how the website performs.

We do not sell your personal information.

How your form submission is handled

When online form delivery is active, your submission is sent to our customer-management system so we can follow up. If online delivery is not active, the form will tell you and ask you to reach us by email or phone instead.

Third-party services

The site loads fonts from Google Fonts and, when enabled, uses Google Analytics 4 for usage measurement. These services may receive limited technical information (such as your IP address) as part of delivering their functionality. We may also use a customer-management platform to receive and manage inquiries.

Data retention

We keep inquiry information for as long as needed to respond to you and, where you become a client, for as long as needed to provide and support our services, unless you ask us to delete it.

Your choices

You can ask us to access, correct, or delete the information you've given us by emailing info@secretsystems.io. You can decline to provide information, though this may limit our ability to respond.

Changes

We may update this policy. The "last updated" date above reflects the most recent version.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email info@secretsystems.io.
