This is the exact automation sequence Secret Systems builds for every client. It runs 24/7, 365 days a year — while you sleep, while you're on jobs, while you're with your family.

Lead Captured

Lead submits form on your website, ad, or funnel. Instantly enters your CRM with full source tracking.

AI Texts in 60 Seconds

AI sends a personalized SMS within 60 seconds — introducing your business and asking a qualifying question.

03
AI Qualifies the Lead

The AI reads replies, scores intent, and asks follow-up questions — filtering serious buyers from tire-kickers automatically.

04
Appointment Booked

High-intent leads get sent a booking link or get scheduled directly. Confirmations and reminders fire automatically.

Leads that don't book immediately enter a long-term follow-up sequence — SMS, email, and call attempts over days and weeks.

All Automation Systems

Every System We Deploy

AI Lead Response Bot

An AI agent that responds to new leads within about a minute via SMS, qualifies them with a short set of questions, and books the appointment automatically — day or night.

  • 60-second response time
  • Natural conversation AI
  • Intent scoring & qualification
  • Automatic appointment booking
  • CRM sync on every interaction

Auto-Dial Lead Follow-Up

Automated outbound calling system that works through your lead list on a scheduled cadence — leaving voicemails, logging outcomes, and triggering next steps automatically.

  • Scheduled outbound dialing
  • Automatic voicemail drops
  • Call outcome logging in CRM
  • Multi-day follow-up cadence
  • DNC list compliance built-in

Voicemail Drop Campaigns

Pre-recorded voicemail messages dropped directly to contacts at scale — no ringing, straight to voicemail. Perfect for promotions, re-engagement, and seasonal campaigns.

  • Zero-ring delivery
  • Custom recorded message
  • Bulk list sending
  • Delivery analytics
  • CRM trigger integration

Missed Call Text-Back

Every missed call on your business line triggers an instant automated text: "Hey, we missed you — when's a good time to connect?" Never lose a lead to voicemail again.

  • Instant text on missed call
  • Custom branded message
  • AI continues conversation
  • Works 24/7 automatically
  • Integrates with any phone system

AI Website Chat Widget

AI chatbot trained on your business — answers FAQs, captures leads, books appointments, and escalates to a human when needed. Works around the clock on your website.

  • Custom AI training on your services
  • Lead capture built-in
  • Appointment booking capable
  • Human escalation logic
  • CRM integration included

Review Request Automation

After each job is marked complete in your CRM, an automated review request fires via SMS and email. Unhappy customers get routed to a private form — protecting your public rating.

  • Job-completion triggered
  • SMS + email combined
  • Smart negative filter
  • Google & Facebook targeting
  • Weekly rating reports

Pipeline Stage Automation

Leads move through your sales pipeline automatically based on behavior and triggers. Tasks, notifications, and follow-up sequences fire at the right stage — every time, without fail.

  • Behavior-based stage moves
  • Auto task assignment
  • Owner notifications
  • Deadline tracking
  • Win/loss analytics
