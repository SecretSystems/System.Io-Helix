Accessibility

Accessibility Statement

Last updated: 17 July 2026

Our commitment

We want everyone to be able to use secretsystems.io, including people who rely on assistive technology. We aim to meet the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA and treat accessibility as ongoing work, not a one-time task.

What we've done

  • Keyboard navigation with visible focus indicators.
  • Respect for the "reduce motion" setting — animations and background effects are turned off when your device asks for it.
  • A native cursor on touch devices and when reduced motion is requested.
  • Labeled form fields and clear, non-deceptive form feedback.
  • Semantic headings and landmarks, and decorative icons hidden from screen readers.

Known limitations

Some areas of the site use rich animation and interactive visuals. We're continuing to improve color contrast, motion handling, and screen-reader support across every page. If you find something that doesn't work well for you, we'd genuinely like to know.

Get in touch

If you hit an accessibility barrier on this site, email info@secretsystems.io or call (337) 258-8818. Tell us the page and what happened, and we'll help and work on a fix.
